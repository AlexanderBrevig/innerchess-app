Privacy Policy for InnerChess

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Introduction

InnerChess ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Game Progress Data: We store your puzzle progress, scores, streaks, and achievements locally on your device.
  • Settings: Your theme preferences and app settings are stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Advertising Data: When you view ads (free version), our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may collect information as described in their privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (free version only)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Process in-app purchases for premium features

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

InnerChess uses Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect and use data as described in their privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy & Terms

In-App Purchases

We use Google Play Billing (Android) and Apple App Store (iOS) for premium purchases. These services handle payment information according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All game progress and user data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

Children's Privacy

InnerChess is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to play.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your stored game progress through the app
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Purchase the premium version to remove all advertisements

Premium Version

When you purchase the premium version:

  • All advertisements are removed
  • No advertising data is collected
  • Your purchase is handled by the app store (Google/Apple)
  • We do not receive or store your payment information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

International Users

InnerChess complies with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR (European Union) and CCPA (California). Your data is stored locally on your device and not transferred internationally.
